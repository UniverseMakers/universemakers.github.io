Meet the team

About Us

Universe Makers is brought to you by a team of astrophysicists from Durham University, the University of Sussex, Imperial College London, and the University of Portsmouth. Our team spans every stage of a research career — from PhD students and postdoctoral researchers to senior academic staff — all working together to bring cutting-edge cosmology and astrophysics to life for visitors of all ages. We are united by a shared fascination with how the Universe came to look the way it does, and by a passion for sharing that science with the public. This exhibit is made possible thanks to the generous support of Durham University and The Ogden Trust.
